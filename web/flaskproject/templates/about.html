Patient Monitoring

The way we are caring our patientsApril 2, 2014

We keep an eye on our patients’ conditions and progress throughout the duration of the treatment. Our services can store the patients’ medical test reports, which our doctors can access anytime they want. n today’s world, everyone is struggling to prioritize their health in their busy lives. The phenomenal popularity of healthcare apps bears witness to the fact that more and more people increasingly rely on technology to take care of themselves and meet their health goals. What’s even more fascinating is that even healthcare professionals are using smartphone health applications. These apps can help patients contact their doctors in case of an emergency without visiting their clinics. Doctors can now remotely access patients’ health data, monitor their progress, and provide real-time guidance. They facilitate appointments, reminders, check-ups, supervision on the go, and offer a host of other benefits.

Making Appointments

Maintaining physical hand-written appointment journals can be quite cumbersome and, in most cases, outdated. Our apps can be used for making appointments, notifying patients and doctors about upcoming checkups, changing schedules, and other essential updates seamlessly.

Popular Features

  • Patient Health Education
    These apps can educate patients about different health conditions by sharing useful, up-to-date information.
  • Diagnosis
    These are important for preventive purposes as they help patients understand their symptoms and call for help as needed.
  • Reminder
    These provide reminders to take medicines, rest, and follow other healthy practices. They are also used for tracking and forming healthy habits like staying hydrated, getting enough rest, etc.
  • Women’s Health
    These include diet and weight loss applications, and they usually come with features like healthy recipes, trackers, and healthy eating guides.
